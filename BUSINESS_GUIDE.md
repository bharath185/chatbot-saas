## Sales Strategy (Get First Customers)

### Target Market Analysis

**Who needs chatbots MOST?**

1. **Restaurants** ⭐⭐⭐
   - Problem: High volume of calls about hours, reservations
   - Solution: Chatbot handles 80% of questions
   - How to find: Google Maps reviews, 1-star reviews mention wait times
   - Pitch: "I can reduce your support burden by 80%"

2. **E-commerce Stores** ⭐⭐⭐
   - Problem: Customer inquiries overload support
   - Solution: Chatbot answers FAQs, tracks orders
   - How to find: Shopify stores in your area, Instagram DMs
   - Pitch: "Handle customer questions 24/7, zero extra cost"

3. **Freelancers/Agencies** ⭐⭐
   - Problem: Client emails asking same questions
   - Solution: Chatbot on website answers before they email
   - How to find: LinkedIn, local business groups
   - Pitch: "Stop wasting time on repetitive client questions"

4. **Coaching/Training** ⭐⭐
   - Problem: Lots of inquiry emails before sign-up
   - Solution: Chatbot qualifies leads
   - How to find: Facebook ads, local searches
   - Pitch: "Qualify leads automatically, only talk to serious ones"

5. **Local Services** (Plumbers, Electricians, AC repair) ⭐⭐
   - Problem: Missed calls = lost jobs
   - Solution: Chatbot takes jobs, books appointments
   - How to find: Google local, ask friends
   - Pitch: "Never miss a customer inquiry again"
